[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook 1]
Kaali raat di siyahi 'ch shining so bright,
Main labhda haan tainu in the neon light.
Tere bullan di khamoshi chhupandi desire,
Main balda haan ishq 'ch like a midnight fire.

[Hook 2]
Palkan di chhanv thalle playing your games,
Main padhda haan akhiyan 'ch unspoken names.
Sahaan di garmi ae out of control,
Main takda haan tainu body and soul.

[Verse 1]
Udd'di zulfan di chhanv ch dark mystique,
Main labhda haan nishaan tera playing hide and seek.
Dhundli roshni 'ch tera chehra lagda divine,
Main pinda haan ishq tera like a bitter wine.
Uchiyan manzilan te nakhra ud-da so high,
Main karda haan koshish reaching the sky.
Agg laggi ae seene 'ch feeling the burn,
Main takda haan raah tera waiting my turn.

[Pre-Chorus]
Sard hawaavan 'ch teri aawaz dindi chills,
Main rukda haan ohna raahan te crossing the hills.
Dil di dhadkan ruk jandi freezing the time,
Main mangda haan tainu committing a crime.

[Chorus]
Ni tu kach de khidone wangu lagdi so rare,
Main karda haan hifazat handling with care.
Tere akhiyan da jaadu karda hypnotize,
Main dubbda haan in your magical eyes.
Mitha zehar ae ishq tera taking my breath,
Main jiyonda haan tere layi dancing with death.
Lukk-chhip ke milna lagda secret affair,
Main karda haan pyar tainu without a care.

[Verse 2]
Dhuen wale tyre chhad-de black marks,
Main karda haan rule the midnight sparks.
Hawa nu cheerdi mashin chaldi with grace,
Main jittda haan har vaari winning the race.
Zehar vargi zubaan teri cutting like a knife,
Main jarda haan nakhre hazard to my life.
Pittal di goli wangu vajjdi ae smile,
Main turda haan tere naal crossing the mile.

Main karda haan pyar tainu without a care.

[Verse 3]
Ohna hanereyan kamreyan 'ch losing my sleep,
Main jagda haan raatan nu emotions too deep.
Main lukaunda haan hanju mere faking a smile,
Main karda haan travel an endless mile.
Khamoshi vadhdi jandi making me numb,
Main sunda haan dil di beating so dumb.
Tutte kach wangu chubhdi ae teri memory,
Main labhda haan khud nu finding my energy.

[Bridge]
Nazran de ishaare karke steal my soul,
Saeed da dil luttna ae tera ultimate goal.
Nede aake sun le meri secret confession,
Main karda haan pyar tainu my true obsession.

[Final Chorus]
Ni tu kach de khidone wangu lagdi so rare,
Main karda haan hifazat handling with care.
Tere akhiyan da jaadu karda hypnotize,
Main dubbda haan in your magical eyes.
Roshni de saaye ch lagdi like a dream,
Main labhda haan tainu following the gleam.
Sadi kahani amar ho jani written in the stars,
Main bhul janda haan duniya healing my scars.
