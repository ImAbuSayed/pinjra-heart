Udd'di zulfan di chhanv ch dark mystique,
Main labhda haan nishaan tera playing hide and seek.
Dhundli roshni 'ch tera chehra lagda divine,
Main pinda haan ishq tera like a bitter wine.
Uchiyan manzilan te nakhra ud-da so high,
Main karda haan koshish reaching the sky.
Agg laggi ae seene 'ch feeling the burn,
Main takda haan raah tera waiting my turn.
