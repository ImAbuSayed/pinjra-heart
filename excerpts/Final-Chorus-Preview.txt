Ni tu kach de khidone wangu lagdi so rare,
Main karda haan hifazat handling with care.
Tere akhiyan da jaadu karda hypnotize,
Main dubbda haan in your magical eyes.
Roshni de saaye ch lagdi like a dream,
Main labhda haan tainu following the gleam.
Sadi kahani amar ho jani written in the stars,
Main bhul janda haan duniya healing my scars.
