Ohna hanereyan kamreyan 'ch losing my sleep,
Main jagda haan raatan nu emotions too deep.
Main lukaunda haan hanju mere faking a smile,
Main karda haan travel an endless mile.
Khamoshi vadhdi jandi making me numb,
Main sunda haan dil di beating so dumb.
Tutte kach wangu chubhdi ae teri memory,
Main labhda haan khud nu finding my energy.
