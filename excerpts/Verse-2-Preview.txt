Dhuen wale tyre chhad-de black marks,
Main karda haan rule the midnight sparks.
Hawa nu cheerdi mashin chaldi with grace,
Main jittda haan har vaari winning the race.
Zehar vargi zubaan teri cutting like a knife,
Main jarda haan nakhre hazard to my life.
Pittal di goli wangu vajjdi ae smile,
Main turda haan tere naal crossing the mile.
